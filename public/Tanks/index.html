Tanks Sandbox Demo




Controls

Move - W A S D

Fire Primary - Left Click

Fire Secondary - Right Click

This demo should only be played on a PC or Mac (not mobile) and ensure that you have enough RAM available. Also note that the first few seconds of this demo may be very laggy, but should subside soon after

This test demo is not intended to represent the finished state. Currently this project is incomplete and still needs much work. Many changes have been made since it's initial phase and so many aspects such as destroying enemies are currently not present. This demo is meant to be a sandbox to play around with the firing, destruction, and AI components of the game. Enemy tanks or the player tank are not destroyable in this demo.
